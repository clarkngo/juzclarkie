🔨 The Artisan's Forge
"The village crafters tend their flames with care,
yet one great hearth sits cold — no fire there.
Within its iron belly, dark and still,
a clutch of little dragons waits to fill
your hands with wonder. Seek the iron maw
that opens wide by handle — not by claw."
📍 Stage 2 of 4  ·  The Nook
🏡 The Hearthside Nook
"The snow may fall and sleigh bells ring,
but heroes don't rest — they slay everything.
Where warmth gathers and fellowship grows,
beside the cold hearth where the winter wind blows.
A party expansion awaits you near —
find the spot where the whole crew convenes for good cheer."
📍 Stage 3 of 4  ·  The High Peaks
🏔️ The High Peaks
"Higher than the forge, beyond the hearthside glow,
the mountain summit calls where only wind can go.
The ruler's chambers crown the keep with grace —
but riches lie concealed within a secret place.
Enter the passage where garments hang in rows,
hidden 'twixt the finery, a treasure no one knows."
